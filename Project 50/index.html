Intresting Facts about Pandas

  • Pandas are born looking like baby badgers.
  • An adult can eat 12-38 kilos of bamboo each day!
  • Pandas like to lick copper and iron. They sure have strange behavior!

Intresting Facts about Flying-Cicadas

  1. Cicadas are the loudest insects known to man. They can be louder than rock concert.
  2. The word "Cicada" comes from the Latin word, meaning "tree cricket"
  3. There are about 3000 known species of cicada worldwide.

Intresting Facts about Teddy Bears

  1. When one bear dies, other members of the family grieve very deepy.
  2. Some species of Asiatic bear build nests in trees.
  3. Their skulls have unusual adaptations to help eat a wide range of food.

Intresting Facts about Camels

  1. Camels have three sets of eyelids and two rows of eyelashes to keep sand out of their eyes!
  2. Mother camels carry their calves up to 14 months before giving birth.
  3. They also have thick lips which let them straw for thorny plants other animals can't eat.

Intresting Facts about Pigeons

  1. Pigeons are incredibly complex and intelligent birds.
  2. Pigeons are known for their outstanding navigational abilities. That's why carrier pigeons were used for letters.
  3. They mate for life and raise two chicks at the same time.

Intresting Facts about Chameleons

  1. Their feet work like salad tongs!
  2. Unlike many lizards, they can't regrow their tails.
  3. Chameleons change color in order to regulate body temperature.
